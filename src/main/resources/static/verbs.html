Verb Learning Tips

  • Conjugation Practice: Regularly practice verb conjugations in different tenses for proficiency.
  • Contextual Learning: Learn verbs in phrases or sentences to better understand usage.
  • Irregular Verb Focus: Pay extra attention to irregular verbs due to their unique patterns.
  • Voice Recording: Improve pronunciation and fluency in verb usage through self-recording.
